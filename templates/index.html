About Fairdressers

This website provides recommendations of hairdressers (and/or barbers) for people who do not want to specify their gender. In many cases such businesses have separate pricelists according to the customer's gender, even if the haircut is the same, making it significantly more expensive when it is classified as a "ladies" haircut. This practice exacerbates the inequality between men and women by making services more expensive in a demographic that is already underpaid. It also frustrates people who do not want to have a style that is consistent with their gender. For example many women do not want a "ladies" hairstyle and end up paying three times the rate for short haircut that is typically considered a male style. The practice of forcing customers to declare their gender also creates a very uncomfortable situation for members of the transgender and non-binary community, who may face hostility if they are not careful about which busisses they choose to frequent.
